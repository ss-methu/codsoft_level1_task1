Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam BR was an Indian aerospace scientist and statesman who served as the 11th president of India from 2002 to 2007. He was born and raised in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu and studied physics and aerospace engineering.
Born: 15 October 1931, Rameswaram
Died: 27 July 2015, Shillong
Awards: Bharat Ratna, Veer Savarkar Award, Hoover Medal

Mary Teresa Bojaxhiu MC, better known as Mother Teresa, was an Albanian-Indian Catholic nun and the founder of the Missionaries of Charity. Born in Skopje, then part of the Ottoman Empire, at the age of 18 she moved to Ireland and later to India, where she lived most of her life.
Born: 26 August 1910, Skopje, North Macedonia
Died: 5 September 1997, Kolkata
